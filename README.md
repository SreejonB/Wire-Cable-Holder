# Wire-Cable-Holder
This is my github repository for my a cable holder. I have everything needed here to make your own cable holder quickly. I made this since my cables always slip away when I unplug my devices and I have to search the wall next to my bed for my wires the next night.
My project is a homemade wire holder that anyone can make out of PLA and a 3d printer. It uses clips that snap on to your wire and hold them onto the base so they do not fall onto the floor or somewhere hard to reach.
It works by snapping the cables you have into the clips and holding them there until you need them again when you un clip them and use the cable like normal. 
I made it since every night when I go to sleep I plug my phone in but most nights I have to stick my arm through the holes in the bed frame to get my wire which hurts quite a bit. It is also very hard to grab and I accidentally unplug the entire brick sometimes which takes forever to plug back in. That is why I made this so I can skip that and just use this every night instead without hurting my arm.
### Step-By-Step Instructions
1.) Print one small and one large clip to test them out on your cables so you know how many of each size clip you need.                                                                                                        
2.) Then you print out however many of each size you need as well as the base.                                                                                                                                        
3.) Once you have everything you take the clips and glue one on each section.                                                                                                                                                  
4.) Then you glue the base onto what ever you want and snap your cables in.
